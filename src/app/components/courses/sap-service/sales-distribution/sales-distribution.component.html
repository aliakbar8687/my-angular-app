SAP Sales & Distribution (SD)

Companies around the world rely on SAP ERP to plan, execute, and analyze their entire sales cycle. The SD module optimizes critical processes like sales, delivery, and billing. With SAP certification in SD, you gain exponential career growth as a consultant or trainer.

I. Within an Organization

As a certified SAP SD consultant, you can join leading firms, often starting as a project manager based on your functional expertise.

II. As an Independent Consultant

As an independent SAP functional consultant, you can support global clients—making your skills highly sought-after.

III. As an SAP Trainer

Most SAP customers need thorough training. With certification and experience, you can step into the role of a professional SAP Trainer.
