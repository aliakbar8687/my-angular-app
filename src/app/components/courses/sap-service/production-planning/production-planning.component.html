SAP Production Planning (PP)

Today, manufacturing companies across the world rely on SAP ERP for better planning and production management. The Production Planning (PP) module automates key processes in the supply chain. SAP customers worldwide seek certified PP consultants with the right skills and expertise. SAP eAcademy’s PP module equips you to efficiently manage manufacturing operations, and certification supports exponential career growth.

I. Within an Organization

As an SAP Certified Consultant, you may be hired by leading firms. Depending on your expertise, you could start as a Project Manager—boosting your career growth significantly.

II. As an Independent Consultant

Working independently as a functional consultant opens opportunities globally, positioning you as a go-to expert for SAP PP solutions.

III. As an SAP Trainer

Many SAP customers require thorough training. With certification and experience, you can also become an SAP Trainer for organizations or training institutes.
