How Syvaxia Began

Syvaxia was founded with a bold vision: to create a lab where technology isn't just developed, but reimagined. What started as a small group of tech enthusiasts has evolved into a global hub for digital innovation.

From Passion to Product

We believe great ideas come from passionate minds. Our journey began in a co-working space, where long nights and caffeine-fueled brainstorming laid the foundation for groundbreaking products and enterprise-grade platforms.

Key Milestones

  • 2018: SAP Training Center established in Dubai
  • 2019: Launched first SAP certification program
  • 2021: Introduced cloud-based SAP training and remote labs
  • 2023: Recognized among Top 10 SAP Training Institutes in GCC
  • 2025: New branch opened in Bangalore, India
